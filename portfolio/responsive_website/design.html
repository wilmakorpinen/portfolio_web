Color scheme

Contemporary style can have a bold starkness–think monochromatic black and white–but they also can swing all across the color wheel. Thats why I chose these basic colors and also this soft brawn and distinct electricity blue.

Typography

I chose humanistic Gill Sans, sans serif family because of its reliability, legibility and versatility. I will use font size: 14px in body text and 24px heading.
